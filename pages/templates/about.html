About White Rose

We are a dedicated team from the Technical University of Sofia, committed to developing innovative solutions that create harmony between nature and technology while establishing autonomous energy zones for sustainable urban living.

Our Mission

Create autonomous energy zones that operate independently of traditional power grids

Foster harmony between nature and technology through biomimetic design

Develop sustainable urban infrastructure that enhances community spaces

Promote circular economy principles through component recycling and reuse

Our Vision

To transform urban landscapes into self-sustaining ecosystems where renewable energy generation seamlessly integrates with natural aesthetics, creating spaces that serve both environmental and community needs.
