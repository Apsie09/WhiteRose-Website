Frequently Asked Questions

How is White Rose powered?

Hybrid solar + wind with smart storage.

Is it suitable for cities?

Yes; low-noise VAWTs and compact footprint.

What about wildlife?

Slow rotation minimizes bird risk; structure can even provide shelter.

Can components be recycled?

Yes; supports reuse of PV panels and turbine parts to cut cost and waste.

What's the rollout plan?

Pilot in 12–18 months; monitor/optimize; scale via partnerships.
